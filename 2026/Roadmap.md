# 2026 DSA Mastery Roadmap: From Fundamentals to Finality

Welcome back to the journey! This roadmap is designed to give you **unshakeable confidence** by balancing deep conceptual understanding with high-volume practice across LeetCode, GFG, and CodeChef.

---

## The 6-Phase Journey

### Phase 1: Foundations & The Toolbelt (Week 1-2)
*Focus: Master your language (C++) and understand how to measure efficiency.*
- **Concepts**:
  - Time & Space Complexity (Big O, Omega, Theta).
  - C++ STL Deep Dive: `vector`, `set`, `map`, `unordered_map`, `priority_queue`, `stack`, `queue`.
  - Memory Management & Pointers (Review from Archives).
- **Practice Strategy**: 
  - Solve "Easy" array problems on LeetCode to get comfortable with STL.
  - Implement basic data structures from scratch.

### Phase 2: Linear Data Structures & Mathematics (Week 3-5)
*Focus: Building logic with arrays and lists.*
- **Concepts**:
  - Arrays: Sliding Window, Two Pointers, Prefix Sum, Kadane's Algorithm.
  - Linked Lists: Singly, Doubly, Circular (Reverse, Cycle detection, Merging).
  - Bit Manipulation: Power of 2, Counting set bits, XOR tricks.
  - Basic Math: Sieve of Eratosthenes, GCD (Euclidean), Fast Power.
- **Goal**: 10 problems per topic on GFG (for variety) and LeetCode.

### Phase 3: Recursive Thinking & Sorting (Week 6-9)
*Focus: Thinking like a computer. Everything in this phase is the foundation for Trees/DP.*
- **Concepts**:
  - Recursion: Base cases, Recursive trees, Tail recursion.
  - Backtracking: N-Queens, Rat in a Maze, Sudoku Solver.
  - Divide & Conquer: Merge Sort, Quick Sort.
  - Binary Search: Lower/Upper bound, Search on Answer (Crucial).
- **Goal**: Solve 20 medium backtracking/binary search problems.

### Phase 4: Hierarchical Structures (Week 10-14)
*Focus: Mastering non-linear data structures.*
- **Concepts**:
  - Trees: Binary Trees, BST, AVL/Red-Black (Theory), Segment Trees (Introduction).
  - Heaps: Min-Heap, Max-Heap, Heap Sort.
  - Hashing: Collision handling, Custom hash functions.
- **Practice Strategy**: LeetCode "Tree" tag is your best friend here.

### Phase 5: Optimization & Connectivity (Week 15-20)
*Focus: The "Hard" stuff. This is where you grow the most.*
- **Concepts**:
  - Dynamic Programming: 1D, 2D, DP on Trees, Bitmask DP. (Start with Memoization).
  - Graphs: BFS, DFS, Dijkstra, Bellman-Ford, Floyd-Warshall, MST (Kruskal/Prim), Topological Sort.
  - Disjoint Set Union (DSU).
- **Goal**: Master the patterns, not just the solutions.

### Phase 6: Competitive Edge (Ongoing)
*Focus: Speed, Accuracy, and Versatility.*
- **Strategy**:
  - **LeetCode**: Bi-weekly & Weekly Contests.
  - **CodeChef**: Participation in Starters and Long Challenges to improve logical stamina.
  - **GFG**: Focus on "PotD" (Problem of the Day) to maintain consistency.
