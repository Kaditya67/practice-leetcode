# 2026 DSA CURRICULUM: The Deep Dive Syllabus

## Why this order?
The curriculum is designed with Dependencies in mind.
- You can't master Trees without Recursion.
- You can't master DP without understanding Recursion and State Space.
- You can't master Graphs without understanding Stacks/Queues and STL.

---

## Topic Syllabus

### 01. Foundations & STL
- Core Vector: Logic behind resizing (O(1) amortized).
- Map vs Unordered Map: When to use which based on data distribution.
- Complexity: Why O(N^2) fails for N > 10^4.

### 03. Arrays & Searching
- Sliding Window: The most efficient way to solve string/subarray problems.
- Binary Search: Not just for finding a number, but for finding a threshold in a monotonic function.

### 06. Recursion & Backtracking
- The Call Stack: Visualizing how memory handles recursive calls.
- Pruning: The art of breaking a recursion early to save time.

### 11. Dynamic Programming
- Memoization: Transforming recursion into efficient lookups.
- Tabulation: Solving things iteratively to avoid stack overflow.

---

## The Finish Line
By the end of this journey, you should be able to solve LeetCode Mediums within 20 minutes and hards within 45 minutes.
