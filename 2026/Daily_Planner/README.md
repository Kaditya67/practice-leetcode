# The Daily Planner

This directory is your personal Accountability Tracker. Use this to plan your days, track what you are actively doing, and log what you have completed.

## 📝 How to use:
1. Every new day, copy `TEMPLATE.md` and rename it to today's date (e.g., `2026-02-24.md`).
2. Fill out your `Plan to Do` list in the morning.
3. Move items to `Doing` when you start them.
4. Move them to `Done` when finished.
5. In the evening, fill out the `Reflection` section.

Consistency is key. Even if you only complete one small task, log it here.
